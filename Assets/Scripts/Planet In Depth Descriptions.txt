Mercury is the closet planet to the sun, its temperature reaching upwards of 800°F. Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system, and is a dense rocky planet.-
Venus is the second planet from the sun and the hottest planet reaching almost 900°F. Venus' surface is covered in volcanoes, and its atmosphere is made of sulfuric acid and carbon dioxide.-
Earth is third planet from the sun, and our home. Earth is made up of 71% water and the only planet to have free oxygen in its atmosphere and the only one known to support life.-
Mars is the fourth planet from the sun. Mars' surface is made up of iron minerals that give it the rust look. Mars has both the highest mountain and the deepest valley in our solar system.-
Jupiter is the fifth planet from the sun and is also the largest planet in our solar system. Jupiter is made up of gases giving it the name gas giant.-
Saturn is the sixth planet from the sun and is famous for having its rings. Because of what Saturn is made up of, it is also a gas giant like Jupiter. Saturn can have winds up to 1000 miles per hour.-
Uranus is the seventh planet from the sun. Uranus' axis is nearly perpendicular to that of the other planets, with it pointing nearly at the sun-.
Neptune is the eighth and final planet in our solar system. Neptune has the fastest winds in our solar system, up to 1500 miles per hour!-
